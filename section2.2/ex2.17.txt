Deï¬ne a procedure last-pair that returns the list that contains only the last
element of a given (nonempty) list:

(last-pair (list 23 72 149 34))
(34)

(defn last-pair [xs]
  (letfn [(find [x xs]
            (if (empty? xs)
              x
              (recur (first xs) (rest xs))))]
    (list (find (first xs) (rest xs)))))
